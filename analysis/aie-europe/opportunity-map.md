## 1. Where the Puck Is Going: Falsifiable Predictions (6-12 Months)

1.  **The Death of the Chat Bubble:** By Q1 2027, "Durable UI" (Canvas/Table/Dashboard) will account for >70% of professional agentic workflows. Text-based chat will be relegated to low-stakes consumer use.
2.  **1200 TPS as the Floor:** Sub-100 TPS models will be considered "batch processing relics" by end of 2026. Real-time, streaming inference (Codex Spark class) becomes the mandatory IDE standard.
3.  **The "Agent-Legible" Refactor:** 30% of top-tier OSS repos will adopt "Agent-Legible" standards (unique function names, flat structures, no "hidden magic") to optimize for token-efficiency and context-coherence.
4.  **Disaggregated Inference Dominance:** The Nvidia/Grok and OpenAI/Cerebras hardware-software splits will become the default cloud architecture. Prefill vs. Decode will be billed as separate line items in AWS/GCP.
5.  **The End of the "Editor":** At least two "Unicorn" startups will mandate a "No-Manual-Edit" policy for all generated code, forcing engineers to work exclusively on the "Harness" (Lints/Tests/Prompts).
6.  **MCP as the Enterprise Gateway:** >50% of Fortune 500 internal "agents" will connect to sensitive data via Stateless MCP Gateways rather than custom API integrations.
7.  **The Junior Dev Gap Crisis:** Entry-level hiring for "coding" roles will drop by 40% as "Dark Factories" (automated agent swarms) absorb all commoditized ticket-closing work.
8.  **Temporal Awareness in Models:** The "o4" class of models will release a "Latency/Fluidity" objective function, allowing agents to reason about UI animation and "human frustration" in real-time.

## 3. What to IGNORE (The Commoditization Trap)

*   **Generic "Chat-with-X" Wrappers:** Low moat, high churn. Chat is the wrong primitive for work.
*   **REST-to-MCP Auto-Wrappers:** "Cringe" (Parra). Agents need intent-based tools, not 1:1 HTTP mappings.
*   **Prompt Engineering Agencies:** Logic is moving into "Markdown Skills" and "Lints-as-Prompts."
*   **Monolithic "One Big Brain" Agents:** Swarms of specialized agents (Best-of-N) outperform generalist monoliths every time.
*   **"Junior Dev" Copilots:** The market is saturated. The value is now in "Full-Job" automation for Senior Architects.

1.  **The "Entropy Explosion" (Ronacher):** Agents generate months of technical debt in days. Without "Agent-Legible" standards, codebases become unreadable even to the AI that wrote them.
